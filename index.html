Welcome at THAT'S AMORE!

Our Restaurant

A real journey for your taste buds


That's Amore is a world of authentic experiences and delicate emotions for the palate. A warm and welcoming atmosphere, where you can enjoy traditional Italian dishes revisited with a modern touch. We offer a menu that changes several times during the year, with a rigorous selection of seasonal products and special attention to the choice of raw materials. We will value and favor local quality products. We collaborate with the best fish importers and select the best cuts of meat.

A love story


That's Amore restaurant is the creation of two dynamic and entrepreneurial young men, Domenico and Leonardo, who share a passion for the culinary world. The name "That's Amore" was inspired by the famous Dean Martin song and celebrates Italian culture abroad. At That's Amore, we aim to showcase our culinary skills and bring a taste of Italy to your table. We believe in the power of love, passion, and respect for the art of cooking, and it shows in every dish we prepare.

So come join us, and let us take you on a journey of authentic Italian flavors, from the comfort of our charming place.

Our Staff

Leonardo

The chef


His name is Leonardo and he is a true lover of cooking. During his travels through various regions of Italy and the world, he has acquired an encyclopedic knowledge of cuisine and culinary culture. His studies in hotel management at one of the most prestigious institutes in Calabria only reinforced his passion for cooking. During his culinary adventures in Florence, Turin and the Canary Islands, he discovered new flavors and inspirations that fueled his culinary creativity. Finally, he moved to Lausanne to make his dream come true: to share his passion for cooking with the world by opening his own restaurant. He will be delighted to welcome you and help you discover the richness of Italian cuisine and culinary culture. He promises a warm and unforgettable culinary experience!
